What's all the hype about Tesla's new pick up truck?

Tesla's new pick up truck, Cybertruck, was revealed last year and it is an out of the ordinary truck, not only because it is electric powered, but because of its out of this world look.

https://www.tesla.com/xNVh4yUEc3B9/04_Desktop.jpg

What separates Tesla's Cybertruck from the rest?

The Cybertruck is an electric powered truck that con go from 0 to 60 miles in 6.5 seconds and has an estimated range of 250 miles per charge, maybe it does not sound very promising, but that is only the base model.

https://www.tesla.com/xNVh4yUEc3B9/08_Desktop.jpg

Here is more information about every Cybertruck trim.

Single Motor RWD Dual Motor AWD Tri Motor AWD

Cybertruck is also built with durability and passenger protection in mind.

  • Exoskeleton
  • Ultra-Hard 30X Cold-Rolled Stainless Steel
  • Tesla Armor Glass

These three design features make Cybertruck the safest pickup truck on the road.
