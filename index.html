What's all the hype about Tesla's new pick up truck?
Tesla's new pick up truck, Cybertruck, was revealed last year and it is an out of the ordinary truck, not only because it is electric powered, but because of its out of this world look.

What separates Tesla's Cybertruck from the rest?
